## 产品介绍

<img src="image/ms9891.png" width="500">

| 中文名称 | 英文名称 | 按键用法 | 
|---------|---------|---------|
| 左键 | Left Button | Click to select or drag.|
| 右键 | Right Button | Click to open context menus.|
| 中键 (即滚轮) | Middle Button (3D Scroll Wheel) | Scroll up and down changer pages or documents. |
| 前进键 | Forward Button | Click to navigate to the next visited page/path in web browsers or folders. |
| 后退键 | Backward Button | Click to navigate back to the previously visited page/path in web browsers or folders. |
| DPI 键 | DPI Button | Click to cycle through different levels of DPI for a preferred sensitivity and precision. <br> If some models support to switch bluetooth channel switch. |
| 模式拨码开关 | Mode Switch | Toggle to switch connection mode between 2.4G wireless mode and Bluetooth (BT) mode. |
| USB口 | USB-C Port / USB-A Port | Connect with USB cable for wired connection or charging the mouse. |
| 红外传感器 | Optical Sensor | Detect mouse movement by projecting light onto the surface and capturing rapid image changes beneath the mouse. |
| 电池仓 | Battery Compartment | Open to put in or change the battery. |
| 接受器存放处 | (USB) Receiver Storage | Open to get out or store the USB receiver. |
| DPI 指示灯 | DPI Indicator | Indicate different levels of DPI during working. |
| 普通指示灯 | Indicator | Indicate working or battery status. |

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
**注释：**
- DPI：每英寸点数，衡量鼠标移动灵敏度和精确度的单位，指鼠标每移动一英寸，屏幕上的光标所移动的像素数。 
- Report Rate：报告率，即鼠标向计算机报告其位置的频率，单位是赫兹 (Hz)。更高的报告率可以带来更平滑的光标移动。
