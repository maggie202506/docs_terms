# Mouse 产品线术语与句库

## 1. 术语

### DPI
- English: Dots Per Inch
- Chinese: 每英寸点数
- Definition: A measure of mouse sensitivity, indicating how many pixels the cursor moves per inch of movement.

### Left Click
- English: Left Click
- Chinese: 左键点击
- Definition: Primary mouse button click.

### Scroll Wheel
- English: Scroll Wheel
- Chinese: 滚轮
- Definition: Wheel between left and right buttons used for scrolling.

### USB Receiver
- English: USB Receiver
- Chinese: USB 接收器
- Definition: USB device that connects the wireless mouse to the computer.
